Evolution of the Game

The game began as a super simple game that I played with some friends in high school. As a Senior in high school I decided to make about 10 cards that looked just liek the inital picture here on the left. However, as time progressed the game developed at an exponential rate. More and more people had become interested in it, and I came up with more and more unique cards.

The game was initially named Island Hopping, but soon was rebranded into Tides of War. However, last year, Disney relut soon was rebranded into Tides of War. However, last year, Disney released Pirates of the Caribean: Tides of War onto the appstore. That forced me to rebrand again to what it is known as today: Tides of Terragos.

I have hosted four different tournaments over the last four years and have had a blast creating, balancing, and testing my card game with friends. If you have any interest in helping me test the game, please contact Harrison Weese at TidesOfTerragos@gmail.com !
